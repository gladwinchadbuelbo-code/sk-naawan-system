# 🌐 PUBLIC TRANSPARENCY PORTAL - ACCESS GUIDE

## **FOR PUBLIC VIEWERS (NO LOGIN REQUIRED)**

---

## **🔗 HOW TO ACCESS THE PUBLIC PORTAL**

### **Option 1: From Landing Page**
1. Open the application in your browser
2. You'll see the **Landing Page** (homepage)
3. Look for the **"Public Transparency Portal"** button
4. Click it to enter the public portal

### **Option 2: Direct URL**
Simply navigate to: **`/public`**

Example:
- If your app is at `http://localhost:3000`
- Go to: `http://localhost:3000/public`

## **❌ WHAT PUBLIC CANNOT SEE:**

These are PROTECTED and require login:

❌ Pending proposals (not yet approved)
❌ Draft reports (not yet submitted)
❌ Internal comments and revisions
❌ Returned/rejected proposals
❌ Staff-only discussions
❌ Edit/delete functions
❌ User management
❌ System settings
❌ Activity logs (staff only)
